What's in the tub

Our ice cream uses fat-free yoghurt to ensure it is as healthy as it can be, from only 76 calories per tub! As dogs cannot digest lactose, we have made the formula lactose-free so there will only be happy tummies over here!

There are two flavours, banana and peanut butter with more to follow soon. Our ice cream is made with 100% natrual ingredients with no added preservatives, sugar or any nasties ❤

Please keep ice cream frozen at -18°C and consume within 5 months of purchase for optimal taste
Please note that while ice cream baseline is fat-free, the peanut butter and bananas have naturally occurring fats.
