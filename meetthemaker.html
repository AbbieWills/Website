About the Maker

Hello everyone!
I have worked in close relation to dogs over the past 6 years and just love everything about them!
During this time, in 2021, I completed my bachelor’s degree in Bio-veterinary Science with a large focus on canine nutrition.

While out walking in Summer 2021, I noticed people were feeding their dogs human vanilla ice cream which got me thinking that it can't be healthy for dog's to be eating human ice cream along with dogs inability to digest lactose. I decided to make my own.

I created my first batch of doggy ice cream for my pet Labrador Pippa which she loved. I then decided to make it for other people’s fur babies which has now brought you to read this .
